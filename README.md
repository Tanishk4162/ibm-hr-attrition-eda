## 📌 Project Overview

This project performs **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on the IBM HR Analytics 
Employee Attrition dataset. The goal is to understand **why employees leave the company** 
by analyzing patterns in age, income, department, and overtime.

This is a real-world HR Analytics use case actively used by companies to reduce 
employee turnover and improve retention strategies.

---

## 💡 Key Findings

- 📌 **~16%** of employees left the company (237 out of 1470)
- 📌 Employees doing **OverTime** are more likely to leave
- 📌 Employees who left had **lower monthly income** on average
- 📌 **Sales department** has the highest attrition rate
- 📌 Most employees are aged between **28 to 40 years**
